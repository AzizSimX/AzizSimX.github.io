Welcome

I am a Master's graduate in Chemistry (specialization: Physical Chemistry), passionate about molecular dynamics simulations and computational chemistry. My interests include protein aggregation, protein-ligand interactions, and developing computational strategies for drug discovery.

About Me

I have experience in computational modeling, data analysis, and advanced use of simulation software (GROMACS, AMBER). My current research involves simulating amyloid aggregation, inhibition mechanisms, and exploring quantum computing approaches to cryptography. I aim to contribute to improving force fields using machine learning and AI, with the long-term goal of advancing computational drug discovery.
