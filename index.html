Welcome

Hello! I am Abdul Aziz Mandal, a researcher in computational chemistry and molecular dynamics simulations.

About Me

I am a Master's graduate in Chemistry (specialization: Physical Chemistry) with a strong interest in computational chemistry. My work focuses on protein aggregation, protein-ligand interactions, and drug discovery using molecular dynamics simulations.

My long-term goal is to integrate molecular simulations with machine learning to improve force fields and accelerate computational drug discovery.

  • Research Training – IISER Kolkata (2022)
